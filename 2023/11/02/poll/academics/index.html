Broken down by

The Herald’s poll found that 33.5% of respondents study physical sciences, followed by social sciences, humanities and arts, and life sciences, the same order as the results of The Herald’s fall 2022 poll. Approximately 3% of respondents were unsure of their concentration. Because the poll allowed respondents to select multiple concentrations, these numbers add up to more than 100%.
