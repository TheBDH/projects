The Brown Bookstore has sat at the heart of Thayer St. for decades.

Though its appearance has changed multiple times since 2000, the Bookstore is among the most consistent storefronts on Thayer — it’s one of just 11 stores from 2000 that persists today on the street.

The Herald went back in time to identify the evolution of Thayer Street — a campus mainstay with an evolving appearance and culture.
