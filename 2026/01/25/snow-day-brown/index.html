The first snow at Brown in photos

On Jan. 25, Providence received around a foot of snow. Classes were canceled for Monday, Jan. 26. Despite weather warnings encouraging students to stay inside, Brunonians ventured out to join in snowball fights, take walks in the fresh snow and breathe the crisp winter air.

The Herald captured photos of the first snow of the second semester.
